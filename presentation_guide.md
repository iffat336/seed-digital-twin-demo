# 🌰 Seed Digital Twin: Interview Presentation Guide

This project demonstrates your ability to bridge **Plant Science** with **Advanced Engineering Math** using **Machine Learning**.

## 🎯 Key Talking Points

### 1. The "Problem" (The Physics)
"In Prof. Garbowski's and Prof. Szymczak-Graczyk's work, you focus on the **Hygrothermal Performance** of materials—how moisture diffusion affects structural integrity. You use numerical **Homogenization** to scale complex materials like AAC or cardboard into manageable models."

### 2. The "Solution" (The Surrogate Model)
"I built this project to show how my **Machine Learning** skills can speed up your specific research. I simulated 1,500 **Hygrothermal-Mechanical** coupled data points based on your non-linear diffusion logic and used them to train an **ANN Surrogate Model**."

### 3. The "Result" (Real-Time Digital Twin)
"My model now acts as a **'Real-Time Digital Twin'**. When sensors detect changes in **Relative Humidity** or **Mechanical Loading**, the AI recognizes the pattern from your math instantly, allowing for a 1-millisecond response for **Smart Farming** monitoring."

## 🚀 Demonstration Steps (The "Showstopper")

### The Streamlit Dashboard
1. Open your terminal in the project folder.
2. Run command: `streamlit run app.py`
3. **During the interview:** 
   - Share your screen and show the dashboard.
   - **Explain**: "I built this interactive dashboard to show the real-time capability of our Digital Twin."
   - **Move the sliders**: Show how high moisture and pressure instantly trigger the Red "Critical Risk" alert.
   - **Highlight the "Inference Speed"**: Point out that it takes less than 1 millisecond. This is why AI is needed to make the Professors' math work for real-world farmers.
